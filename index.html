Factors Affecting Maturity Amount

Several factors affect the maturity amount, including:

  • Policy Tenure: Longer tenures may yield higher maturity amounts.
  • Premium Payments: Higher premiums generally result in a bigger maturity amount.
  • Bonus Rates: Depending on LIC's performance, bonuses may vary.
